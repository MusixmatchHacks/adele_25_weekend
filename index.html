Search and lyrics engagement trends: Adele's 25

By Varun Jewalikar and Federica Fragapane at Musixmatch

Adele's '25' was released last Friday, 20th November. Let us look at some interesting search and listening/reading (lyrics) trends for the album over the weekend.

Lyrics dominate 'Adele 25' search trends

Google trends is an interesting tool for comparing google search volumes of various terms. Even though the exact volume of searches is not specified but we can still make some interesting relative comparisons.

Searches on the last weekend

This image shows that the searches start peaking out on release date which could indicate the listeners looking for lyrics, music and videos of Adele's new album.

The lyrics search volume is 2 times higher than 'adele 25 torrent' and 'adele 25 youtube'. Listeners are searching far more for the lyrics than the music or videos for Adele's 25 on release day.

Searches over the last 2 months

Lyrics searches dominate over music and video searches for Adele's new album over the last two months.

On October 23rd the first single from the album 'Hello' was released and we see a massive jump in all the searches. The lyrics search trend is more than 3 times higher than 'adele 25 torrent' and 1.5 times higher than 'adele 25 youtube'.

Torrents to the rescue of streaming subscribers ?

This interesting trend was spotted by Andy Weissman. The torrent searches start peaking out on release date. One reason could be that Adele chose to not release the album on streaming services which causes the listeners to search for alternatives elsewhere.

Musixmatch engagement for Adele's 25

Musixmatch's apps are amongst the most used music apps worldwide. Also, our website is one of the top lyrics sources on the internet. From both of these we gather a lot of listening/lyrics reading data (anonymised) every minute. Here we zoom in on this data over the last weekend for Adele's new album.

Overall

From 19th November to 22nd November, our users have spent 4364 hours (~182 days) reading this album's lyrics from our apps and website. This figure shows the total time spent reading the lyrics over this period.

Track & platform wise Musixmatch engagement for Adele's 25

The engagement in our apps is much higher than our website. The engagement everyday over the weekend remains almost constant (~ 1100 hours on the apps and ~50 hours on the website).

'Hello' is by far the more listened song from this album right now on both the platforms. This is closely followed by the second single 'While we were young' on the apps, while on the website 'Send my love' is more popular. The other 8 songs are still to gain significant listening.

Conclusion

Adele's fans were eagerly waiting for this album since 2011, the huge search and Musixmatch engagement patterns indicate that this could indeed be a very popular album. The lyrics search trends for this album dominate over music and video searches which is driving a lot of engagement on our website and apps.
